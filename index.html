Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CaretEDA

What is CaretEDA? +
CaretEDA is an AI-powered EDA platform designed to help chip designers reduce errors, speed up verification, and streamline silicon development using open-source tools.
Who is CaretEDA for? +
Our tools are built for chip designers, semiconductor companies, and EDA engineers who want to leverage AI to improve design efficiency and reduce costly re-spins.
Is CaretEDA open source? +
CaretEDA is open-source enabled, meaning it integrates with and supports open-source EDA tools and workflows. Parts of our platform are available on GitHub.
How can I get early access? +
You can request early access by filling out the contact form below or emailing us at info@careteda.com. We will get back to you within 2 business days.
